The title of this site is MTG Deck Sorter, representing that it does just that, sorts some decks and grabs the relevant info

The purpose of the site is to showcase the decks I have built as well as incorporate API usage to retrieve data from them, rather than rely on an exhaustive prewritten array.

Scenarios: What benefit might the have for the average person? If they are looking to build decks of a similar type, then they have something to look through, especially if they're looking for specific subtypes for those deck themes. What happens if they dislike or disagree with some of the deck makeup? That's their right to do so, and they can leave their opinion in the box that will be at the bottom of the page.

Color Schema and Typography are included on the CSS
